WHAT ARE SYNTHETIC STONES?

A synthetic stone is lab-grown, designed to look just like its natural counterpart, which can take thousands of years to form. Synthetic stones share the same chemical, optical and physical traits as their natural counterparts.Synthetic stones tend to have fewer color variations (although slight variations can be found in lighter-colored stones like pink and purple), considerably reducing material waste when producing jewelry pieces. Synthetic stones have a key difference from man-made stones.
